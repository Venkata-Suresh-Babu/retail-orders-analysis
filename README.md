# Retail Orders Analysis | SQL & PostgreSQL

## 📊 Project Overview
A comprehensive data analysis project leveraging PostgreSQL to uncover actionable business insights from 9,988+ retail orders. This project demonstrates proficiency in data cleaning, transformation, and advanced SQL analytics to drive strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- Clean and validate large-scale retail datasets for accuracy
- Engineer financial metrics (profit, revenue, discount, sale value) through calculated columns
- Identify high-performing regions, categories, and shipping modes
- Analyze year-over-year growth trends and seasonal patterns

---

## 📈 Key Findings
✅ **2023 profit growth of +18.7%** compared to 2022  
✅ **New York leads revenue** at 22.3% of total  
✅ **Technology category drives 34.1%** of total profit  
✅ **Standard shipping dominates** 78.5% of Office Supplies & 62.4% of Furniture orders  
✅ **Total Revenue:** $11.06M | **Total Profit:** $204.8K  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Language:** SQL
- **Data Source:** Kaggle (Retail Orders Dataset)
- **Data Volume:** 9,988 records across 16 attributes

---

## 📋 Analysis Performed
- Year-wise and monthly sales trends by category
- Top 10 performing cities by revenue
- Top 10 products by sales volume
- Top 5 best-sellers per region
- Shipping mode impact on profitability
- Category-wise monthly performance
- Growth comparison analysis (2022 vs. 2023)
